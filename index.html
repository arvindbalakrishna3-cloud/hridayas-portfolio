About Hridayas

Crafting Timeless Spaces

Founded in 2018, Hridayas is a premier architecture and interior design firm based in Chennai, India. We specialize in creating harmonious living spaces that blend traditional Indian aesthetics with contemporary design principles.

Our team of architects, designers, and craftsmen work closely with clients to understand their vision and transform it into reality. We believe that every space tells a story, and our mission is to create environments that resonate with the soul of Indian culture while meeting modern lifestyle needs.
